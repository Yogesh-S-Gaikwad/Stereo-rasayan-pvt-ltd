Who we Are ?

Stereo Rasayan Pvt. Ltd. is a leading manufacturer and innovator in the field of nanothech and chemical solutions. Specializing in advanced nanoparticle chemicals, the company is dedicated to revolutionizing crop care and enhancing productivity. Stereo Rasayan Pvt. Ltd. is at the forefront of producing high-quality nanoparticle-based chemical solutions. These cutting-edge products provide precise and efficient solutions for pest control, plant health improvement, and soil fertility enhancement. A flagship product of Stereo Rasayan Pvt. Ltd., Nenotech Zetine is a specialized formula designed to promote plant growth and strengthen resistance against environmental stresses. Its nano-level efficiency ensures maximum absorption and effectiveness.

The company also manufactures premium-grade abscisic acid, a plant hormone crucial for regulating growth, stress responses, and seed dormancy. Stereo Rasayan Pvt. Ltd. ensures superior quality and consistency in its abscisic acid production.
